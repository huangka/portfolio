About

I'm a UX/UI designer, occasional scribbler, and avid reader.

My journey into the realm of user experience design was a natural one. The field of user experience allowed me to channel my passions for the arts and literary worlds by providing a conduit for creativity, critical thinking, and stepping into the shoes of others'. Since then, I've been hungry for opportunities to uncover problem spaces, pinpoint creative opportunities, ideate innovative solutions , and design experiences that have meaning and have the potential to enrich and impact a user's life in some capacity.
